GOD The Almighty has created approximately 84,00,000 living creatures and the humans are considered to be the most intelligent of them all. It is a wonderful observation to note that almost all the creatures in the Animal Kingdom do have all the five senses in common. The sense of vision, hearing, smell, taste and touch. Today, we the humans are so dependent on our sensory organs that it is almost impossible to think of leading a good life without any of them. In this segment we will try and restrict our discussion to the sensory organ related to hearing.

The American Association of Audiologists (AAA) had conducted a worldwide survey with a very sizeable sample and the following were some of its finding

  1. The world is infested with approximately 8% of human population who are hard of hearing due to various reasons
  2. This figure is growing at an alarming pace of almost 2% every 5 years
  3. These numbers are by and large spread evenly across the globe
  4. 60% of that number is attributed to pediatric patients (children below the age of 6 years). However it is believed that a vast majority of these patients could have done better had their problem been diagnosed and early medical intervention was made available to them. Ignorance and social stigma are still a few of the large forces to reckon with
  5. The AAA further reports that while the degree of awareness is pretty high in some of the advanced countries, the science and technology required to address the issue is still found wanting. However today instrumentation technology has grown leaps and bounds and when combined with the techniques and skills of the trained audiologists, the world can be made a better place to live

Our Vison and Mission

To provide and facilitate the access to the best of hearing aid technology to every patient hard of hearing by providing state of the art hearing aids after proper diagnosis.

Organisation

On the 6th of February 2022, four likeminded people got together to address and contribute their bit to help people with problems in hearing from all age groups Viz., pediatric, geriatric and the youth alike. Smt. Bhavana Anoop, Smt. Ranjana Swamy, Smt. Vasuki Hindupur and Sri. Prabhakar Kulkarni under the name and aegis of Manomay Hearing Aid Center (a unit of New Sree Industries).

Objective

To make Manomay Hearing Aid Center the best center of its kind in terms of reach and service to patients.

It is our endeavour to make Manomay Hearing Aid Center a clinic which would provide specific solutions to those in need and more importantly create a very high level of awareness on problems relating to hearing disorders vehemently among the newborns, infants and toddlers. We at Manomay Hearing Aid Center believe in our slogan.

HEAR BETTER LIVE BETTER
